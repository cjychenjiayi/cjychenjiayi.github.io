I am **Jiayi Chen**, a PhD researcher in Computer and Information Engineering at the **Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen**, advised by Prof. Guangxu Zhu and Prof. Shuai Wang. My research sits at the intersection of **embodied intelligence**, vision-and-language navigation, reliable planning, and wireless sensing.

I am interested in how robots turn underspecified language into goals they can justify, reach, and execute. Since May 2026, I have been working with **AgiBot** on VLN and query-conditioned map evidence, including real-robot validation across floors on the KuoTuo D2 Max. The current line of work is being prepared for an ICRA submission.

# 🔥 News
